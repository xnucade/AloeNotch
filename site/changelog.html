Changelog

AloeNotch is actively developed. Here's what's changed.

0.2.0 — July 19, 2026

  • Song scrubber. Now Playing shows a live progress bar with elapsed / total time — drag it to seek.
  • The shelf remembers. Staged files now persist across launches, and a new Drag all handle pulls every file out at once.
  • Settings window. A proper preferences window: toggle each module, launch at login, and nudge the panel's horizontal position.
  • Tighter layout. The expanded panel is more compact and better spaced for the content it holds.

0.1.0 — July 17, 2026

  • First release: a Dynamic Island-style panel that hugs the MacBook notch and expands on hover.
  • Now Playing from any app — Apple Music, Spotify, and even YouTube in a browser — with transport controls and an ambient glow drawn from the artwork's color.
  • Drop shelf for staging files, plus calendar, weather, battery, and a live clock at a glance.
  • Menu-bar switchboard to show or hide each module.

Follow along or file ideas on GitHub.
